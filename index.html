Let’s get to know the variables.

Move your cursor over each variable to learn what it means.

q q means heat energy. It is measured in joules (J). A positive q means the system gained heat. A negative q means the system lost heat. = m m means mass. In this tutorial, mass will be measured in grams (g). c c means specific heat capacity. In this tutorial, it will use units of J/g°C. ΔT ΔT means change in temperature. It is found by calculating final temperature − initial temperature. In this tutorial, it will use °C.
